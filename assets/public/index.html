About Hangover

Hangover is a webrtc based peer to peer video chat. It's free. It creates you an unique URL that you send to your peers and that's it.

Make sure your browser supports all this webrtc stuff. Limits may apply depending on the browser implementation.

We use the great SimpleWebRTC library.
